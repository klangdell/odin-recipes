Savory Goulash

Goulash is a dish that originated in Hungary. It is a dish that consists of tomatoes, pasta and ground beef and the best part is that it is quick and easy to make!

The Goulash that I make is made with onions and diced tomatoes. It is the absolute perfect dish for a cozy night in or that cold autumn weather

Either way, this dish is a great meal that should be added to your dinner rotation.

Ingredients

    1lb of elbow pasta
    1 large yellow onion
    2 cans fire roasted diced tomatoes
    1lb of ground beef
    1/4 tsp of salt
    pepper to taste
    Step 1: Boil pasta until al dente or tender
    Step 2: While pasta is cooking, brown your ground beef
    Step 3: While ground beef is browning, dice up the onion and add it to the ground beef
    Step 4: Add in your salt and pepper. Be sure to stir everything around
    Step 5: Add in your diced tomatoes and cook on low until everything is heated up
    Add your pasta to the beef and mix until all ingredients are combined
