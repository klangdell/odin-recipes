Cozy chicken and dumplings

Another cozy dish to have in the fall is Chicken and Dumplings. This is great for fall and winter and is a nice meal to add into your dinner rotation

Ingredients

    1 pack of 4 count chicken thighs
    2 stalks of celery
    2 cups of flour
    1 32oz Chicken Broth
    3-4 cups of water
    1/4 tsp salt
    1 tsp pepper
    1 tsp italian seasoning
    First boil your chicken broth. While you're waiting for it to boil, cut up your celery.
    Once the broth is boiling add in your italian seasoning, salt, pepper, celery and finally your chicken thighs.
    Cover and let cook for 30-35 minutes on low-medium heat
    Once the chicken has thuroughly cooked, remove and add to a plate to cool down
    Next add your flour into a large mixing bowl, followed by the water. Mix until thick and sticky like
    Add a teaspoon size scoop of batter and drop it into the broth
    Keep adding the batter until the top of the pot is covered in dumplings. Let cook for 10 minutes
    While dumplings are cooking, shred up the chicken and add to the pot. Stir, serve and enjoy!
