Spicy Ramen Noodles

This dish I came up with on my own, inspired by all of the ramen noodle cooking videos I have seen on TikTok.

It's spicy but not too spicy. It's filled with a lot of umami flavors and comforting

Ingredients

For the broth you will need

    2 tbsp fried chili flakes in oil
    1 1/2 tsp sesame seed oil
    1 tbsp red curry paste
    1 carton beef bone broth
    1 1/2tsp organic miso
    1 tsp garlic paste
    10 drops of fish sauce

produce needed

    shitake mushrooms fresh or freeze dried
    green onions
    bamboo shoot
    fresh ginger root
    wheat ramen noodles
    Step 1: Add your sesame seed oil and chili flake oil in a large wok. If you dont have a wok, a pan is fine
    Stir until combined, then add your curry paste. Let cook for about 2 minutes
    Step 2: Add the beef bone broth, fish sauce, miso and garlic paste. At this step, shred up about half a teaspoon of your fresh ginger
    Step 3: Add your mushrooms, the whites of the green onions and bamboo shoots
    Step 4: Finally add your noodles and cook noodles until tender. Garnish with the green on the green onions.
